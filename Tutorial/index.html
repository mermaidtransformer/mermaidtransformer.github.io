If you made it that far and informed yourself enough,
is here a tutorial for the transformation

To become a mermaid or a merman, you have to get completely wet by pouring water
over yourself or bathing in chlorine-free water during the full moon.
Then, you have to hold your hands up towards the full moon for 10 to 20 minutes.


Here are a few more tips so it might work better:

  • Use natural water, such as water from a lake or stream or, of course, the ocean.
  • Wear a piece of jewelry, such as a necklace, during the ritual.
  • Some sources also claim that you should hold your hands "V-shaped" to the full moon.
  • It is also said to help if you swim or dive for half an hour afterwards
    or think very hard about mermaids. Of course, this works best
    if you try it together with a friend and then talk about it.
  • Besides, most transformations have happened in the sea,
    but you almost never hear about "pool mermaids". :)
